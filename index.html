Widgets & Tools

  • 🔨
    Route Builder: Use this tool to piece together a Route ID by selecting County, System, Route, etc., if you do not already know it.
  • Find Measure: Calculate ranges or specific locations along a route. You can enter Milepoints, GPS coordinates, or City names/Addresses. Leaving the Start and End references blank will highlight the entire route.
  • Address Search: Zoom the map directly to a specific street, address, or city.
  • Bridge Lookup: Find bridges by Bridge Number (e.g. 20-77-103.65) or BARSID (e.g. 20A403).
  • Layers: Toggle the visibility of different map layers on or off.
  • Basemaps: Switch the background map between Aerial Imagery, Topographic, and Vector street views.
  • Legend: View the symbology logic for the routes and bridges on the map.

Map Interaction Tips

  • Click any road on the map to query it and display the Route ID, Street Name, and exact Measure data.
  • When viewing route details, click the "Details" button to load full RIL Database attribution.
  • Hit Esc or click anywhere outside this dialog to dismiss it.
